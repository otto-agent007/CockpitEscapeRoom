# Presentation Benchmark: DC-9 Simulator Coverage

Source: Simulation Daily article about a DC-9 package for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024/2020.

## Prototype Requirement

- Dense analog flight deck should feel materially rich from the captain seat.
- Cockpit lighting should include restrained integral, dome, map, and flood-light impressions.
- Major movable items such as wipers, windows, doors, knobs, switches, handles, and stairs are useful reminders that visible mechanisms matter.

## Later Enhancement

- More animated cockpit controls beyond puzzle-critical switches.
- Window reflections and glass depth.
- Floor, briefcase, and map-light mood details.
- Cabin or exterior glimpses after the main cockpit reads correctly.

## Intentionally Out Of Scope

- Flight-simulator systems depth.
- Realistic engine, hydraulic, electrical, fuel, pressurization, fire, or ice-protection procedures.
- Takeoff, warning, GPWS, or emergency audio.
- Flight dynamics, weight and balance, or real manuals.
- Using simulator screenshots as authoritative DC-9-51 geometry.
